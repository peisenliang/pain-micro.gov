Royal Decrees of PAIN

All laws, orders, and imperial commands issued by the Sovereign of the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation shall be displayed below, in full and without abridgment. May the flippers of justice never falter.

Royal Decree No. 001 — Succession Protocol

Date Issued: 31/07/25

Whereas the continuity of the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation (PAIN) and the dignity of its sovereign office are of paramount importance; and

Whereas the preservation of rightful succession safeguards the stability and honor of the realm;

It is hereby ordained as follows:

1. Upon the demise of the reigning Emperor Penguin, a thorough and impartial investigation carried out by “Penguin Guards” and two Puffins shall be conducted to determine the circumstances surrounding said event.

2. Should evidence arise implicating any individual named within the Emperor’s Last Will and Testament in the Emperor’s untimely demise, such individual shall be irrevocably disqualified from succession to the throne or any claim thereof.

3. In the absence of culpability ascertained through investigation, succession shall proceed according to the following order of precedence:
   a. The Empress Penguin, if duly designated;
   b. The Eldest Prince or Princess of the Imperial Line;
   c. A duly appointed member of the Arch Puffin Council.

4. In the unlikely event that all aforementioned successors are found culpable or otherwise incapacitated, the sovereignty of PAIN shall be transferred to an allied Micronation, on the condition that the penguin populace shall continue to flourish unhindered within the absorbing nation.

Let this decree stand as the unassailable foundation for the governance of succession within the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation, ensuring order, justice, and the eternal prosperity of the realm.

By my imperial authority,  
Emperor Penguin Pei Sen Liang  
Sovereign of PAIN

Royal Decree No. 002 — Governance Protocol

Date Issued: 03/08/25

Whereas the structure and clarity of authority are vital to the continued sovereignty and internal function of the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation (PAIN); and

Whereas it is the right and responsibility of the Crown to establish the governing framework under the eternal chill of our dominion;

It is hereby ordained as follows:

I. The Monarch

1. The Emperor Penguin shall possess absolute authority over all state, civil, cultural, and military affairs.
2. All decrees, edicts, and decisions made by the Emperor shall be final, save for voluntary appeal as granted by the Crown.
3. The throne shall remain indivisible, sovereign, and unchallenged in perpetuity, save for succession outlined in Decree No. 001.

II. The Council of Puffins

1. The Council shall serve as the primary advisory body to the Emperor Penguin.
2. The Council shall be composed of the following tiers of representation:

    a. The Arch Puffin — Senior-most advisor and keeper of tradition.
    b. The Puffin of Defence — Overseer of homeland protection and Arctic security.
    c. The Puffin of War — Commander of all ceremonial and defensive military operations.
    d. The Ambassador Puff — Chief diplomat, foreign correspondent, and liaison to other micronations.
    e. The Puffin of Ice & Infrastructure — Responsible for internal development, architectural integrity, and environmental oversight.
    f. The Puffin of Culture — Oversees public festivals, symbolic identity, language, and media representation.

3. The Council shall not possess legislative power but may petition the Emperor with recommendations, warnings, or proposals.

III. The Penguin Guards

1. The Penguin Guards shall serve as the ceremonial militia of PAIN, protectors of the realm, and investigators under imperial command.
2. They shall be trained in non-lethal deterrents, crowd organization, and the ceremonial flipper salute.
3. Membership in the Penguin Guards shall be voluntary and earned through honor, trial, or imperial appointment.

IV. The Citizenry — Nestlings

1. All citizens of the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation shall be known formally as Nestlings.
2. Nestlings shall be entitled to the protection, cultural access, and symbolic representation afforded by the Crown.
3. Citizenship shall be granted upon declaration, ceremony, or approved application.

Let this decree stand as the definitive structure of governance within the Penguinize Arctic Island Nation. Henceforth, all authority shall be conducted within this cold, unyielding framework, under the gaze of the eternal auroras.

By my imperial authority,  
Emperor Penguin Pei Sen Liang  
Sovereign of PAIN
