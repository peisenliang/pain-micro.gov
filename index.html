Penguinize Arctic Island Nation (PAIN)

Motto: “We ruled in the Ice Ages, we’ll rule again.”

Welcome to PAIN — a frozen beacon of chill sovereignty, penguin culture, and symbolic might in the Arctic Circle. We are a micronation founded on icy independence, warmth in community, and the unstoppable waddle of progress.

Core Values & Philosophy

  • Resilience: Like icebergs, we endure the ages.
  • Adaptability: Inspired by penguins, we thrive in extremes.
  • Revolutionary Warmth: Fire burns in our coldest places.

Government & Leadership

  • Form: Absolute Monarchy
  • Sovereign: [Emperor Penguin Pei Sen Liang](https://peisenliang.github.io) (Evan Liang)
  • Heir Apparent: TBD — the next Queen Penguin or chosen successor awaits
  • Council of Puffins: Cultural, environmental, and diplomatic stewards

Capital

Iceclaw Harbor: A glacial capital built on geothermal sustainability, carved in ice, and sculpted in penguin form.

Languages

  • Penguinese: Symbolic and ceremonial
  • English: Used for all diplomatic, admin, and communication needs

Currency

The Iceflake (IFK): Symbolic internal currency of PAIN. No crypto — just cold, hard identity.

National Holidays

  • Penguin Day (Jan 20): Celebrating PAIN’s founding with fish feasts and ice-slide races
  • Fire & Ice Festival (June 21): Bonfires, glacier swims, and the celebration of duality

Diplomatic Stance

Friendly neutrality. PAIN extends diplomatic flippers to all. We pursue symbolic recognition, environmental alliances, and Arctic research partnerships.
